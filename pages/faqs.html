Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Farmpalm.

Farmpalm is the online Marketplace for West Africa's Palm Oil Supply Chain.Farmpalm is a digital platform that connects farmers,aggregators, processors, and buyers across the palm oil supply chain.

Farmers gain access to live pricing, digital harvest records, better market visibility, and improved bargaining power. They network with like-minded professionals in the field and grow their business.

Registration is free for farmers and buyers, however the free plan is limited. Additional, affordable packages are available to optimise business.

Buyers can use Farmpalm's traceability features and QR tracking to verify product origin and supply chain data.

Farmpalm operates on a self-tailored, market-based pricing system to make ascertain fair pricing and corruption- free transactions. Business background checks are carried out before approving suppliers.

All the major languages in West Africa are supported to make it easier for users.
