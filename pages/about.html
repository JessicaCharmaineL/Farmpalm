Why Farmpalm Exists
Africa produces over 60% of the world's palm oil yet the smallholder farmers who grow the crop see less than a fraction of its value. Middlemen dominate pricing information. Buyers can't verify what they're purchasing. Processors operate on guesswork. And farmers have no digital record of anything they've grown or sold.
Farmpalm exists to rebalance this. We are building transparent, accessible digital infrastructure that gives every farmer — regardless of their location, literacy level, or phone type — the same market access that large corporations have always had.
