Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember user preferences, improve performance, and provide a better browsing experience.

2. How Farmpalm Uses Cookies

Farmpalm uses cookies and similar technologies to ensure the website operates correctly, maintain security, remember user preferences, and improve platform performance.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. Without them, certain services and features may not be available.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website, allowing us to improve performance and user experience.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember settings and preferences such as language choices and other user-selected options.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as analytics, authentication providers, payment processors, or cloud hosting platforms that may place cookies on your device in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage, disable, or delete cookies through browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality and user experience.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with the updated revision date.
